== Description ==

Be compliant with the Cookies and GDPR Legislation with HOB Advanced Cookies.

HOB Advanced Cookies allows you to give your users the choice to block the usage of non-essential cookies such as facebook, twiiter, youtube... Your site will respect and be compliant with GDPR.

What is GDPR? 
The General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 is a regulation in EU law on data protection and privacy for all individuals within the European Union and the European Economic Area. It also addresses the export of personal data outside the EU and EEA areas.

== Installation ==
1. Go to you administration area in WordPress `Plugins > Add`
2. Look for `advancedcookies` (use search form)
3. Click on Install and Activate the plugin

= FTP Method =
1. Upload `advancedcookies` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to use HOB Advanced Cookies after installation? =
In the Configuration tab, you can customize button text, colors, cookie bar position, delay before displaying (in seconds) and duration of consent (in days). 
In the Service Management tab, you can write a description for each service.
